Our Mission

Founded by Afrikaan Sahra[1], a refugee who hopes to change lives through advocacy, business and technology[1], Tech4Refugees is committed to harnessing the power of technology to transform the lives of displaced people. We develop programs that promote digital literacy, connect refugees with educational resources and open pathways to entrepreneurship.
