Our Programmes

At Tech4Refugees we design programmes that address the diverse needs of displaced communities. From hands‑on workshops to advocacy campaigns, our initiatives are unified by the belief that technology can help everyone thrive.

Digital Literacy & Education

We provide accessible training in computer basics, coding, and digital literacy. Through partnerships with universities and tech companies, participants gain skills that enhance employability and open doors to further education.

Advocacy & Awareness

Our advocacy campaigns amplify refugee voices and challenge stereotypes. We host online discussions, create multimedia storytelling projects and collaborate with media outlets to highlight the talents and contributions of refugees.

Community & Entrepreneurship

Technology can spark entrepreneurship. We mentor refugee entrepreneurs, provide micro‑grant opportunities and connect innovators with mentors and investors to help them turn ideas into sustainable ventures.
